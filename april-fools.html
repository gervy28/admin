Website Tip Day 4 of 30: Keep Your Passwords Secure

Password security and mismanagement is a major threat to the security of your business! There are a few basic rules to abide by when it comes to password management.

  • Never share your password with anyone else.
  • Do not use the same password across multiple accounts.
  • Don't use passwords with common words
